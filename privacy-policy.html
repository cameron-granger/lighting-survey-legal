Lighting Survey Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lighting Survey ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Lighting Survey iOS app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information needed to provide lighting survey workflows for field teams and managers.

  • Account and identity data: Sign in with Apple user identifier, name/email (if shared), company assignment, role.
  • Survey and operations data: location details, survey records, issues, recommendations, sign-off details, and attached photos.
  • Device and diagnostics data: sync status and support snapshot data when users explicitly export it.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users and manage role-based access.
  • Create, sync, and manage lighting survey workflows.
  • Generate reports and support sign-off processes.
  • Maintain reliability, security, and auditability.
  • Respond to support and troubleshooting requests.

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • Data may be stored locally on device.
  • Cloud-synced app data is stored in Apple CloudKit services associated with the app.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with authorized users in your organization, Apple services required for app operation, or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as needed to provide functionality and support business records.

  • In-app account deletion removes the user profile and local account namespace for that account on device.
  • Some operational records may remain when required for company workflows, audit trails, or legal obligations.

6. Your Choices

  • Sign out at any time.
  • Initiate in-app account deletion from the account menu.
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion support.

7. Children

Lighting Survey is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date above reflects the current version.
